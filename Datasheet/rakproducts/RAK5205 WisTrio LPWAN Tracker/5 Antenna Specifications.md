### GPS Environmental Requirements

The antenna environmental requirements are listed in the table below:

| **Conditions** | **Temperature** | **Humidity** | 
| ---- | ---- | ---- | 
| Working | -35 °C ~ +80 °C | 0% ~ 95% | 
| Storage | -40 °C ~ +85 °C | 0% ~ 95% |
